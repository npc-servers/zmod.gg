PRIVACY POLICY

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last Updated: September 25, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services, including our website, game servers, and related features.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Account Information

When you use our services, we collect:

  • Steam ID and associated public profile information
  • IP addresses used to connect to our services
  • Discord user ID and associated public profile information (when using our Discord server)

2.2 Communication Data

We collect and store:

  • Messages sent in our game servers
  • Messages sent in our Discord server
  • Reports and support tickets

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to our game servers and services
  • Maintain server security and enforce our rules
  • Prevent abuse, cheating, and unauthorized access
  • Investigate and address rule violations
  • Communicate about service updates and changes

4. DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure data transmission and storage
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training and oversight

5. DATA SHARING

We may share your information with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our services
  • Law enforcement when required by law
  • Other players (limited to public profile information)

6. PAYMENT PROCESSING

For store purchases and transactions, we partner with payment processors including:

  • PayNow - A payment processing service specializing in game server monetization
  • Tebex - A game server monetization platform

When making purchases, you will be directed to these services' platforms. Each service has its own privacy policy and terms of service that govern how they collect and use your information. We encourage you to review their respective policies before making purchases.

7. YOUR CHOICES

You can control your privacy through:

  • Steam privacy settings for your profile information
  • Discord privacy and visibility settings
  • Choosing what information to share in chat messages

8. DATA RETENTION AND ACCESS

Due to the nature of our services and how users connect to them:

  • We retain basic connection data (IP addresses, Steam IDs) for security and operational purposes
  • Chat messages and communication logs are stored for moderation and security purposes
  • We cannot provide complete copies of user data or fully delete historical data
  • Some data retention is necessary to maintain server security and prevent abuse

9. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

10. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Our services are operated from the United States. If you access our services from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries.

11. CHANGES TO PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through our website or email.

12. CONTACT INFORMATION

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact us through our Discord server or support channels.

By using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to our Privacy Policy.
