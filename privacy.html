PRIVACY POLICY

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

We collect information that you provide to us or that we access through authorized third-party platforms when you connect to our servers, participate in our communities, or make purchases on our store.

Personal Information

  • IP address
  • Steam ID and associated public profile information
  • Discord user ID and associated public profile information (when using our Discord server)

Payment Information

When you make purchases, payment data is processed and stored by our payment processors (PayNow and Tebex), not directly by us:

  • PayNow
  • Tebex

Technical Data

  • Log and Usage Data: Connection logs, error logs, device information, browser information, and activity data
  • Device Data: Information about the device you use to connect to our services
  • Location Data: General location based on IP address (typically your ISP's location, not your exact location)

Communication Data

  • Messages sent in our game servers
  • Messages sent in our Discord server
  • Support tickets and reports

2. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Enforce our rules and security measures
  • Prevent cheating, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Investigate rule violations
  • Process payments through our payment processors
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Communicate service updates
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. INFORMATION SHARING

We only share your information in limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate our services
  • To comply with legal obligations (court orders, subpoenas, etc.)
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and the public

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Authentication: Steam, Discord
  • Payments: PayNow, Tebex
  • Analytics: Google Analytics, Cloudflare

4. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS

Your rights vary based on your location. Users in the European Economic Area have additional rights under GDPR. If you believe we are processing your data improperly, you may contact your local data protection authority:

  • EU Data Protection Authorities
  • Swiss Data Protection Authority

5. ACCOUNT INFORMATION

You do not have a direct account with ZMOD.GG. Your profile information exists on third-party platforms:

  • To modify Steam information: Update your Steam privacy settings
  • To modify Discord information: Update your Discord privacy settings

To stop our collection of your data, simply discontinue using our services. We may still retain certain information as required for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect website functionality.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain data as needed for:

  • Security and operational purposes
  • Community moderation
  • Fraud prevention
  • Legal compliance

Due to the nature of our services, we cannot provide complete copies of all user data or fully delete historical data in many cases.

7. CONTACT US

For privacy questions or concerns, please contact us through our Discord server or support channels.
