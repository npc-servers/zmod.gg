PLATFORM GUIDELINES

Maintain a positive environment for all players. Follow our comprehensive guidelines to ensure everyone has a great experience on our servers.

  1. Use common senese.
  2. Hate speech in any form is strictly prohibited. This includes langugage, dog whistles, images and references, and any other form of expression that is intended to harm or discriminate against someone based on their race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, or other protected characteristics.
  3. Keep chat, media, and in-game actions appropriate. Although lewd and sexual langauge is acceptable in some context, overtly sexual language, actions, and content are not. No NSFW content in any form is allowed, including violent or harmful content.
  4. No spamming, excessive caps, or excessively disruptive behavior. Micspam is acceptance in certain contexts, but if you're told to stop, stop.
  5. No advertising other servers, websites, or Discords. This includes in the DMs of our users. Do not share malicious links, files, or anything that could be considered harmful or illegal.
  6. No sharing of personal information (yours or others). We even recommend you not share social media accounts of non-celebrities, our thirdparty platforms (like Discord) may constitute this as Doxxing or harassment.
  7. No discussion of sensitive topics, like politics, wars, mass shootings and other tragedies.
  8. Keep discussion of drugs, guns, and piracy to a minimum.
  9. No sharing of leaks.
  10. Be respectful to other players. Harassment, bullying, and witch hunting is strictly prohibited.
  11. Avoid interpersonal drama. If you have a problem with someone, please take it to a private message or a support ticket.
  12. We are an english speaking community. Please use english in all communication.
  1. Do not spam in voice and text channels.
  2. Use proper channels for what you're doing.
  3. Follow the Discord Community Guidelines and Terms of Service.
  1. Follow the rules outlined on the server's rules page.
  1. No cheating, exploiting, or using unauthorized modifications. Some clientside Workshop modifications are allowed.
  2. Your platform avatar, username, and bio must be appropriate and not offensive.
  3. Do not trade or sell in-game items for real-world value. Our platforms are not intended for trading.
  4. Play on our servers with one account.
  5. Do not impersonate staff members or other players.
