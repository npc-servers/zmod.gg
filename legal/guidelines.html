COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

Maintain a positive environment for all players. Follow our comprehensive guidelines to ensure everyone has a great experience on our servers.

  1. Respect all players and staff members at all times
  2. No harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
  3. Keep chat appropriate and family-friendly
  4. No spamming, excessive caps, or disruptive behavior
  5. Follow staff instructions immediately and without argument
  6. No advertising other servers, websites, or services
  7. Do not impersonate staff members or other players
  8. No sharing of personal information (yours or others)
  1. No cheating, exploiting, or using unauthorized modifications
  2. No griefing, trolling, or intentionally ruining others' experience
  3. No camping spawn points or AFK farming
  4. Respect roleplay scenarios and server-specific rules
  5. No mic spam, soundboard abuse, or disruptive audio
  6. Follow the intended game mode objectives
  7. No team killing or friendly fire unless permitted
  8. Report bugs and exploits to staff instead of using them
  1. No building in restricted areas or blocking pathways
  2. Keep builds reasonable and not excessively large
  3. No building structures that lag the server
  4. Clean up after yourself - remove unused props
  5. No building offensive or inappropriate structures
  6. Respect other players' builds and property
  7. Follow server-specific building guidelines
  8. No building traps or structures designed to harm others
  1. Use appropriate channels for their intended purpose
  2. No NSFW content, inappropriate images, or explicit material
  3. No excessive pinging of staff or other members
  4. Keep discussions relevant to the channel topic
  5. No sharing of pirated content or illegal material
  6. Respect voice channel etiquette and don't interrupt others
  7. No self-promotion or advertising without permission
  8. Follow Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines
  1. First offense: Warning or temporary mute/kick
  2. Second offense: Temporary ban (1-7 days depending on severity)
  3. Third offense: Extended ban (1-4 weeks)
  4. Severe violations: Permanent ban without appeal
  5. Appeals can be made through Discord support tickets
  6. Staff decisions are final and not subject to public debate
  7. Ban evasion will result in permanent IP ban
  8. False reports may result in punishment for the reporter
  1. Homigrad: Follow RP rules, no RDM, respect the lore
  2. Horde: No camping, work as a team, follow objectives
  3. Sandbox: No excessive prop spawning, clean up after yourself
  4. Mapsweepers: No blocking paths, respect other players' space
  5. Check individual server rules in their respective channels
  6. Some servers may have additional specific guidelines
  7. When in doubt, ask staff for clarification
  8. Server-specific rules override general rules when applicable
