FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I join a server?
Click the "Connect" button next to any online server. You'll need Garry's Mod installed via Steam to join.
What are the server requirements?
You need Garry's Mod on Steam (PC only). Most servers require Counter-Strike: Source content for textures.
How do I report a player or bug?
Join our Discord and open a support ticket. Our staff typically respond within 5 minutes.
Can I play on mobile or console?
No, our servers are PC-only and require Steam. Garry's Mod is not available on mobile or console platforms.
What benefits do supporters get?
Supporters get VIP perks like priority queue, exclusive content, special ranks, and access to supporter-only events across all servers.
